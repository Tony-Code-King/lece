Contract Work

Limpopo EarthMoving Construction Equipment (Pty) Ltd (L.E.C.E) is a leading provider of construction contract works and load and haul services in the Limpopo province of South Africa. With a strong commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction, we have established ourselves as a trusted partner for various construction projects.

Our range of services includes ground preparation, trenching, compacting, and other earthmoving activities. We have a well-maintained fleet of construction equipment, including excavators, bulldozers, graders, loaders, and dump trucks, ensuring that we have the right machinery for any project requirement.

Ground preparation is a crucial step in any construction project, and our team of experienced professionals is well-equipped to handle it efficiently. We have the expertise to clear the site, remove obstacles, level the ground, and ensure a solid foundation for the construction work ahead.

Trenching is another specialized service we offer, which involves digging trenches for various purposes such as laying utility lines, drainage systems, or underground cables. Our skilled operators use advanced equipment to excavate trenches of varying depths and widths, ensuring precision and accuracy.

Compacting is an essential process to ensure the stability and durability of the construction site. We utilize state-of-the-art compactors to compress soil, gravel, or asphalt, creating a solid base that can withstand heavy loads and prevent settlement issues in the future.

At Limpopo EarthMoving Construction Equipment, we understand the importance of meeting project deadlines and delivering high-quality results. Our team is dedicated to providing efficient and reliable load and haul services, ensuring that materials are transported safely and on time.

We prioritize safety and adhere to all relevant industry regulations and standards. Our operators are trained in the proper handling and operation of construction equipment, promoting a safe working environment for everyone involved.

With a strong focus on customer satisfaction, we strive to exceed expectations and build long-term relationships with our clients. We work closely with project managers, contractors, and other stakeholders to ensure effective communication and collaboration throughout the construction process.

Whether it's a small-scale development or a large-scale infrastructure project, Limpopo EarthMoving Construction Equipment has the expertise and resources to deliver exceptional construction contract works and load and haul services. Contact us today to discuss your project requirements and experience our commitment to excellence firsthand.

More Information About Our services

Hitachi Construction Machinery is a leading manufacturer of construction equipment, including excavators, wheel loaders, dump trucks, and compact construction machinery. They offer a wide range of attachments that can be used with their equipment to enhance their capabilities and versatility.

Various types of buckets are available for different applications, such as general purpose buckets, heavy-duty buckets, rock buckets, and ditch cleaning buckets.

These attachments are used for breaking rocks, concrete, and other hard materials. They are commonly used in demolition, quarrying, and construction projects.

Grapples are used for handling and sorting materials like logs, scrap metal, and waste. They come in different configurations, such as clamshell, orange peel, and sorting grapples.

Rippers are used for breaking up hard ground and removing obstacles. They are commonly used in earthmoving and mining applications.

Augers are used for drilling holes in the ground for various purposes, such as installing fence posts, planting trees, or setting foundations.

Compactors are used for compacting soil, gravel, or asphalt. They are commonly used in road construction and landscaping projects.
