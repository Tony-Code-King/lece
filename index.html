Who We Are

About Us

L.E.C.E, an acronym for Limpopo EarthMoving Construction Equipment (Pty) Ltd, stands as a reputable source of top-tier construction equipment solutions not just in the Limpopo Province, but also in surrounding regions. Holding an official distributorship and dealership for Hitachi Construction Machinery, L.E.C.E represents dependability, operational efficiency, and value for money. Above all, the firm places great emphasis on safety, environmental stewardship, and sustainable practices in its activities.
